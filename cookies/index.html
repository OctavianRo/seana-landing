Cookie Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how seána (“we”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on seana.ie. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, to remember your choices, and to provide information to site owners about how their site is used.

How we use cookies

We use a small number of cookies. We group them into two categories:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function and to remember your cookie choice. These are always active and do not need your consent.
  • Non-essential cookies — used for analytics and to understand how the site is used. These are only set after you click “Accept” in our cookie banner.

seana_cookie_consent Essential Remembers whether you accepted or declined non-essential cookies, so we don’t ask again. 12 months
Analytics cookies Non-essential Help us measure visits and understand how the site is used. Only set if you click “Accept”. Up to 24 months

Your choices and consent

As an EU/EEA visitor, non-essential cookies are only used with your consent. When you first visit, a banner lets you Accept or Decline non-essential cookies. Declining means only essential cookies are used.

You can change your mind at any time:

  • Use the button below to reset your choice and show the cookie banner again.
  • Clear cookies for seana.ie through your browser settings.

Managing cookies in your browser

Most browsers let you view, block, and delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect how the site works. For more information, see your browser’s help pages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
